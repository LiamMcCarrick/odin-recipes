Protein Oatmeal

Protein Oatmeal is one of the easiest and most filling breakfast meals to have.

It is a perfect meal for the athlete who is looking for a filling protein heavy meal. You can also add fruit to hit those micronutrients.

Ingredients

  • Rolled oats
  • Protein powder flavor of choice
  • Peanut Butter

Steps

  1. Measure out half a cup of rolled oats.
  2. Add 1 cup of water to the oats and place in the microwave for 2 minutes and 15 seconds.
  3. Add 2 tablespoons of peanut butter.
  4. Add 1 scoop of the protein powder
  5. Mix together and enjoy
