Oreo Truffle

Home

These truffles are deliciously rich, perfectly chocolatey and the addition of cream cheese just makes them sing. Who new something so incredibly easy could be so incredibly tasty?

This dessert is one of my personal favorites to make as it tastes extremelty good and is very easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Oreos
  • Cream Cheese
  • Bar of Bakers Chocolate

Steps

  1. Line a baking sheet with wax paper
  2. Put 36 oreos in a food processor and crush until they are fine crumbs
  3. Add block of cream cheese to crushed oreos and mix until well blended
  4. Scoop out dough and form into 1 inch balls
  5. Place truffles in freezer for 15 minutes. Crush 3 more oreos
  6. Melt bakers chocolate
  7. Remove truffles from freezer and dip in melted chocolate
  8. Sprinkle crushed oreos over the top of truffels
  9. Store in freezer for 2 hours to harden the melted chocolate
